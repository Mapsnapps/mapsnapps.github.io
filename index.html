Data Governance Framework.

Delivering a succesful data strategy relies upon a solid Data governance framework anchored around people, processes and systems. Below are some of the key enablers to meeting the requirements of the strategy.

See more.

Asset Data Policy.

The Data policy should state the intentions & commitments to unlock the full value of data by ensuring it is Accurate, Consistent, Secure, Accessible & Managed. It also details the commitments of data owners and stewards to ensuring the upkeep of data to these criteria.
